北京“Too Yeah！” 探戈节特别邀请了韩国、上海、北京三地的8位DJ，坐镇7场狂欢舞会，他们是——

贺小白 (Julian Bai He)

上海 Shanghai

中国最炙手可热的DJ之一，长驻上海。曾多次受邀前往布宜诺斯艾利斯、罗斯托夫、海参崴、首尔、大阪、新加坡、台北、香港等不同大洲的诸多城市的探戈节和舞会活动DJ，并广受舞者和大师们的好评。他偏爱黄金年代的传统探戈音乐，但并不完全拘泥于此。他通过对音乐的不同能量的把握来调动舞者的热情。他认为探戈是一种灵魂的舞蹈，舞会DJ的任务就是使用音乐把舞者们的灵魂能量释放出来！

Julian Bai He is a traditional tango DJ whom based in Shanghai. He is one of the most popular DJ in China now. He was invited to perform in many festivals and tango events located in some big cities in different continents such as Buenos Aires, Rostov, Vladivostok, Seoul, Singapore, Osaka, Taipei, Hong Kong, and so on. As a famous DJ, he received a lot of praises from many dancers and masters in this field. He loves traditional tango music in Golden Era, but not stick to that. He can always arouse dancers' enthusiasm because he is very good at grasping energy from different type of music. Tango, in his opinion, is the dance with soul, a good DJ should always releasing the energy from the dancers deepest soul by music.

Ben Jamin lee

首尔 Seoul

他是一名传统的阿根廷探戈DJ，同时也是一名富有激情的探戈舞者。出于对阿根廷探戈全身心的热爱，他从2011年开始担任探戈DJ，经常在韩国首尔最受欢迎的舞会上担任DJ。并曾多次受邀担任首尔、新加坡、香港、台北、东京、悉尼、北京、上海、胡志明、米兰、新奥尔良、符拉迪沃斯托克、里加、波尔图、利沃夫、罗兹以及维也纳等多个城市的阿根廷探戈节、阿根廷探戈马拉松等大型国际活动的DJ。他热爱传统的黄金时代风格探戈音乐，并坚持不懈地为舞者挑选适合且充满能量的音乐。他一直努力在每个特定时刻播放合适的音乐以表达他对主流乐团杰出乐曲的尊崇以及对舞会上每一位舞者的尊重。他坚信，好的音乐有助于舞者自然、舒适地沉浸在情感的流动中，从而更加享受在舞会中的时刻。

He is a traditional tango DJ and also an enthusiastic social tango dancer. He started tango DJ since 2011 and regularly plays music at the popular milongas(Tango O Nada and Ataniche) in Seoul Korea. He has been invited as a DJ for various international tango festivals, marathons and encuentros from many cities such as Seoul, Singapore, Hong Kong, Taipei, Tokyo, Sydney, Beijing, Shanghai, Ho Chi Minh, Milan, New Orleans, Vladivostok. Riga, Porto, Lviv, Lodz, and Vienna. He loves the traditional Golden Age tango music and tries to select the songs suitable for dancing with good energy. Respecting the great songs of main popular orchestras and the emotion of dancers in milonga, he tries to play the right tandas for each certain moments. He believes it helps dancers to be naturally and comfortably immersed in the flow of emotion.

Ricardo Tan

上海 Shanghai

作为北京的第一代探戈DJ代表人物。Ricardo 曾经见证了首都探戈圈的成长过程。14年回沪之后，他迅速成为了一名非常受欢迎的上海DJ。Ricardo对于探戈舞曲的编排理念来自于他自始至终渴望服务于舞者，为他们带来最美妙的舞曲体验。传统黄金年代的经典探戈音乐是他的基准。偶尔在合适的时机、为合适的人群他也会涉足于前后年代的音乐。

Traditional Tango DJ
Starting his career as a “Musicalizador” in Beijing in 2010, Ricardo has both witnessed and influenced the early tango DJ scene in the Chinese capital city. After relocating to Shanghai in early 2014, he quickly became a sought-after tango DJ for the local milongas. Ricardo's work is driven by the desire to serve the floor with the most danceable and enjoyable arrangements. Golden Age tango constitutes the strong baseline of his repertoire with occasional excursions into both earlier and later years, played at the right moment and to the right people. Apart from entertaining the regular Shanghai milongas, he also enjoyed working for international tango events in Asia.

昊子 (DahaoZ)

北京 Beijing

昊子是一位富有激情与活力的DJ，同时也是狂热的探戈舞蹈的爱好者。他的音乐刚柔相济，时而行云飘渺，时而又苍劲有力。他懂得在正确的时间、正确的地点为舞者放出他们最需要的音乐。昊子曾受邀担任多个探戈节的DJ，如探戈世锦赛中国赛区大舞会、友城之间海峡两岸探戈周末舞会，及北京各大探戈大师营表演舞会等。

HaoZ is a passionate tango dancer and DJ. His selection of music always keeps the energy flow on the dancing floor. He has been guest DJ at various international festivals, including Mundial De Tango China 2014, Beijing Tango Holiday, and at local milongas in Stockholm and Beijing.

王萌 (Meng Wang)

北京 Beijing

王萌是为舞者而生的DJ, 身为一名资深的Tango舞者, 他以给予舞者节奏感,有能量,充满情感的音乐为傲. 他的快节奏的但是有些时候却又是非常浪漫的, 以非常经典音乐的特征创造出一系列的身心震动让所有的舞者不肯离开舞场,欢跳Tango一夜一夜…
他的最早的音乐灵感直接扎根那些Tango大师的音乐性. , ChichoFrumboli, 曾经指导王萌很多.自生涯开始，曾经DJ许多国家和地区的许多Tango节，比如：　新加坡，美国－洛杉矶，波多黎各，蒙特利尔，多伦多，西雅图，温哥华，棕榈泉

Meng is a DJ for the dancers. Being a very active social dancer himself allover the world, he takes pride in providing rhythm, energy as well as emotionaldepth to the dancers on the floor. His up-tempo but sometimes romantic DJingstyle features the most popular classic orchestras generates a exciting vibefor the dancers and often keeps them dancing non-stop through all-nighters. Hisearly DJ influence came from non-other than the Maestro of musicality himself,Chicho Frumboli, who spent a lot of time lecturing to Meng on how to play theright music for the dance floor. Meng Wang has been the DJ for internationaltango festivals such as Singapore, Los Angeles, Puerto Rico, Montreal, Toronto,Seattle, Vancouver, Palm Springs, as well as Shanghai.

Hagoon

天安 Cheonan

他与阿根廷探戈结缘于2008年，在学习阿根廷探戈两年后，于2010年开始师从Mercury Park, Carlos Lee学习DJ相关内容。目前，他活跃于韩国各个城市的阿根廷探戈活动中，定期在首尔，大田，青州，天安舰的舞会中担任DJ。
Hagoon更偏爱那些浪漫并且富有应有利的黄金时代的音乐风格，他希望在他担任DJ的时候可以给大家提供一个可以忘我地沉浸其中，尽情交流情感，表达并释放情绪的探戈氛围。

He Has been dancing tango since 2008, and DJ-ing since 2010, learned DJ-ing Skill from Mercury Park, Carlos Lee.Currently, Regular DJing in Seoul, Daejeon, Cheongju, Cheonan City Milonga and Tango O Nada.
Hagoon prefer the romantic and impactful music of the Golden Age. and hope people will be able to communicate their emotions when DJing.

Victor

北京 Beijing

北京，致力DJ三载，DJ于自己是文章。情感、能量、细节、起承转合等等，一个都不能少。好的文章一定是越读越吸引人的，好的音乐编排也是。音乐是舞者间交流的语言。会尽最大努力，将此语言合为作品，一点点呈现，温暖每一位到此的人。

Tango DJing is just like writing - the creation of sentimental narratives, the attention to literary attainments and the power to make differences.
Great literature walk their readers through the lines, so does the mindful sequence of tango songs in and between the tandas.
Tango music connects one dancer to the other, emotionally and physically. My enthusiasm is tell a nostalgic, romantic or inspiring story through various composers, band and era.

昊天 (Haotian)

北京 Beijing

北京新晋DJ，“探戈音乐”小程序创立者。他是一名活跃的舞者，乐于探索自己的特长与风格。他同时也热衷分享音乐，自2015年起开始探戈DJ生涯，通过音乐表达自己对探戈的理解。他的音乐富有能量，风格创新又不失传统，敢于尝试不同乐团的音乐，能够持续激发舞者对跳舞的渴望，引领舞者享受丰富的音乐感受。

Haotian is a new tango DJ based on Beijing, begins his career from 2015, the creator of the "Tango Music" mini App. He is an active dancer as well as a practitioner to expands possibilities and explore styles. He is also a passionate DJ willing to share music and express his understanding of tango through music. His music style is innovative with traditional tastes, filled with energy, bold enough to try music from different contemporary orchestras, stimulates dancers' desire to dance throughout the milonga, also able to lead dancers enjoy the depth and richness of Tango.
